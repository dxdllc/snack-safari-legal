Thank you for playing Snack Safari, developed by DXD LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used when you play the Game.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to operate the Game, display advertising, support features, and improve the user experience. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

A. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game displays advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (such as model and operating system)
  • Ad interaction data (such as ad views and clicks)
  • Advertising identifiers provided by the device

This information is processed by Google in order to deliver and measure ads. Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

B. Game Center

If you sign in with Apple Game Center, Apple may process:

  • Player ID
  • Display name
  • Scores and achievements
  • Cloud save data

We do not access or store this information directly, and it is managed entirely by Apple. Apple’s Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

C. In-App Purchases (StoreKit)

If you make an in-app purchase, Apple may process information such as transaction details and purchase status. We never receive credit card information or payment credentials.

D. Device Motion

Certain gameplay features use device motion (accelerometer and gyroscope). This information is used only during gameplay and is not stored or shared.

E. Local Game Data

The Game stores some information locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress
  • Coins, gems, power-ups, and items
  • Settings and preferences

Some data may sync to your Apple account through Game Center.

2. How We Use Information

We use information collected in the Game to:

  • Operate and improve the Game
  • Display advertising
  • Support in-app purchases
  • Provide leaderboards and achievements
  • Maintain performance and stability

3. Third-Party Services

The Game may interact with the following service providers:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform (if shown)
  • Apple Game Center
  • Apple StoreKit
  • Apple SKAdNetwork

These services may collect or process information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

Most data is processed securely by Apple and Google. We use standard measures to protect any data stored locally on your device.
